## One-Sentence Thesis

After the first AI winter, the field's center of gravity shifts toward
probabilistic reasoning, benchmark-driven machine learning, and renewed neural
credibility as researchers learn more from data and less from hand-built rules
alone.

## Why This Era Matters

- it explains why AI becomes more data-driven after the limits of hand-built
  symbolic systems become hard to ignore
- it shows that modern AI grows out of both probabilistic learning and renewed
  neural-network work rather than one clean replacement story
- it creates the direct transition from the first winter to deep learning

## Core Narrative

Era 5 should be taught as a methodological transition, not just as a waiting
room before deep learning. Researchers increasingly lean on probabilistic
reasoning, machine-learning benchmarks, larger corpora, and empirical evaluation
because those methods handle uncertainty, noisy data, and pattern recognition in
ways hand-built symbolic systems often do not. Statistical NLP, speech work, and
pattern-recognition tasks all help normalize the idea that performance can come
from learning regularities in data rather than from manually encoding every
relevant rule.

Neural networks re-enter the story inside that broader change. The 1986
backpropagation revival matters because it restores credibility to multi-layer
learning, but the era should not pretend that the field immediately becomes deep
learning. The better claim is narrower: renewed neural methods join a wider
statistical turn, and by the late 2000s they increasingly look like a serious
path forward because data, compute, and layered representations start to align.

## Transition In And Transition Out

- transition in:
  Era 4 shows why symbolic and knowledge-engineered systems narrow under
  pressure; Era 5 begins when researchers increasingly turn toward data,
  probability, and learned patterns as more scalable tools.
- transition out:
  Era 6 starts when deep architectures, representation learning, and large-scale
  training stop looking like promising alternatives and start becoming the field's
  central performance story.
