# DeepMind

## Name

- name: DeepMind
- institution type: AI research lab and company, later part of Google DeepMind
- active period or relevant date range: founded in 2010; especially relevant to this slice from the 2010s through 2026

## Why It Matters

DeepMind matters because it shows that the modern era is not only about public
chat interfaces. Its trajectory from deep reinforcement learning and AlphaGo to
AlphaFold and Google DeepMind makes the current AI period legible as a story
about general systems, scientific discovery, and large institutional research.
