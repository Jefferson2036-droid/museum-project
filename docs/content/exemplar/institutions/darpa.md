# DARPA

## Name

- name: DARPA
- institution type: government research funding agency
- active period or relevant date range: especially relevant to this slice from the 1960s through the 1980s

## Why It Matters

DARPA matters because AI history is partly a history of which kinds of research
states are willing to fund. In this slice, DARPA helps expand early AI research
capacity, supports projects such as robotics and planning, and later becomes a
sign of how institutional patience narrows when ambitious programs under-deliver.
