# MIT Artificial Intelligence Laboratory

## Name

- name: MIT Artificial Intelligence Laboratory
- institution type: academic research lab
- active period or relevant date range: founded in 1959; especially relevant to this slice from the late 1950s through the 1980s

## Why It Matters

MIT's AI Lab makes early symbolic AI legible as an organized research culture.
It concentrated talent, programming practice, robotics work, and ambitious views
about machine intelligence in one influential institutional setting.
