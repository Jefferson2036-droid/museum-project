# Allen Newell

## Name

- name: Allen Newell
- lifespan: 1927-1992

## Role In AI History

Newell is central to the early symbolic era because he helps define AI as a
field of problem solving, heuristic search, and computational reasoning.

## Why This Person Matters

- he helps make Dartmouth's agenda operational through early programs
- he keeps the story tied to actual systems rather than only manifesto language
- he links AI history to cognitive problem solving as well as engineering

## Major Contributions In Plain Language

- co-created Logic Theorist, one of the first landmark AI programs
- co-developed General Problem Solver and the language of heuristic problem
  solving
- helped establish the view that symbolic systems could model important aspects
  of intelligent reasoning
