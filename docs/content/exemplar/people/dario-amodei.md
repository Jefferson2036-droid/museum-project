# Dario Amodei

## Name

- name: Dario Amodei
- lifespan or active period: active from the 2010s through the modern AI era

## Role In AI History

Amodei is a key bridge between frontier-model scaling and the attempt to make AI
safety, interpretability, and responsible scaling central institutional concerns
of the current era.

## Why This Person Matters

- he helps explain why Era 7 includes a safety-first institutional path and not
  only a public-deployment path
- he makes the modern period legible as a struggle over how capability progress
  should be governed and interpreted
- he links frontier-model development to Anthropic's explicit rhetoric of
  reliability, steerability, and safety science

## Major Contributions In Plain Language

- co-founded and leads Anthropic as a frontier-model company organized around
  safety, reliability, and interpretability claims
- became one of the clearest public figures arguing that scaling and safety have
  to be treated as part of the same historical problem
- helps anchor the current era's responsible-scaling and deployment-governance
  conversation
