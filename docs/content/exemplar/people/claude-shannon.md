# Claude Shannon

## Name

- name: Claude Shannon
- lifespan: 1916-2001

## Role In AI History

Shannon connects logic to switching machinery, formalizes information, and helps
shape the Dartmouth formation moment.

## Why This Person Matters

- he makes the story of AI more than a purely abstract mathematical narrative
- he connects circuits, information, and machine intelligence questions

## Major Contributions In Plain Language

- linked Boolean logic to relay circuits in 1938
- formalized information theory in 1948
- contributed to early machine-intelligence thinking around chess and Dartmouth
