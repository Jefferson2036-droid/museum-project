# Ada Lovelace

## Name

- name: Ada Lovelace
- lifespan: 1815-1852

## Role In AI History

Lovelace matters because she helps frame what symbolic machines might do while
also setting an early limit on how far that claim should go.

## Why This Person Matters

- she gives the history a human voice at the point where symbolic procedure and
  machine possibility start to converge
- she helps make later debates about machine intelligence more teachable

## Major Contributions In Plain Language

- wrote influential reflections on Babbage's engines and their symbolic
  potential
- raised a lasting question about whether following rules is the same as originative thought
