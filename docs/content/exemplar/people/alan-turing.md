# Alan Turing

## Name

- name: Alan Turing
- lifespan: 1912-1954

## Role In AI History

Turing anchors the exemplar slice by defining computability and later asking
what it would mean for a machine to count as intelligent.

## Why This Person Matters

- he gives the era a rigorous starting point for machine computation
- he connects formal reasoning to later questions about machine intelligence

## Major Contributions In Plain Language

- modeled effective computation in 1936
- helped make machine intelligence a serious research question in 1950
