# Edward Feigenbaum

## Name

- name: Edward Feigenbaum
- lifespan: 1936-2025

## Role In AI History

Feigenbaum represents the expert-systems turn, where symbolic AI proved most
effective in narrow, knowledge-rich domains.

## Why This Person Matters

- he gives Era 4 a human anchor for knowledge engineering and expert systems
- he helps show that symbolic AI adapted rather than simply vanished after early
  critique
- he links the field to practical scientific and industrial applications

## Major Contributions In Plain Language

- helped lead DENDRAL, a landmark knowledge-based system for scientific
  inference
- became a key public advocate for expert systems and knowledge engineering
- showed that explicitly encoded expertise could produce useful AI in bounded
  domains
