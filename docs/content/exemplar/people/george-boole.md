# George Boole

## Name

- name: George Boole
- lifespan: 1815-1864

## Role In AI History

Boole gives the prehistory of AI a symbolic language for reasoning that later
becomes essential to computation and logic in machinery.

## Why This Person Matters

- he helps make reasoning look formal rather than purely intuitive
- he gives later eras a way to connect symbolic procedure to machine operations

## Major Contributions In Plain Language

- developed an algebra of logic that helps turn reasoning into symbolic structure
