# Charles Babbage

## Name

- name: Charles Babbage
- lifespan: 1791-1871

## Role In AI History

Babbage matters because he turns mechanized calculation into a serious machine
design problem rather than a loose thought experiment.

## Why This Person Matters

- he gives the prehistory of AI a machinery-centered imagination
- he helps students see that symbolic procedure and machine design have a long
  shared history

## Major Contributions In Plain Language

- designed calculating engines that make mechanized symbolic procedure easier to
  imagine concretely
