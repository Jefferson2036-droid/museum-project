# Yoshua Bengio

## Name

- name: Yoshua Bengio
- lifespan or active period: active from the 1990s through the modern AI era

## Role In AI History

Bengio is one of the clearest anchors for representation learning and the idea
that deep models matter because they learn useful levels of abstraction.

## Why This Person Matters

- he helps explain why deep learning is about learned representations, not only
  bigger parameter counts
- he gives the Era 6 story a conceptual throughline from deep architectures to
  later language models
- he completes the human triad most associated with the deep-learning era

## Major Contributions In Plain Language

- advanced research on deep architectures and representation learning
- helped frame deep learning as the study of models that learn multiple layers
  of useful abstraction
- co-authored the 2015 deep-learning review that stabilized the era's language
  for students and non-specialists
