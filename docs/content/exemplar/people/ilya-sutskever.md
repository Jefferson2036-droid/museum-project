# Ilya Sutskever

## Name

- name: Ilya Sutskever
- lifespan or active period: active from the late 2000s through the modern AI era

## Role In AI History

Sutskever is a key bridge figure from the deep-learning breakthrough period into
the foundation-model era because his career runs directly from Geoffrey
Hinton's Toronto lineage through Google Brain to OpenAI.

## Why This Person Matters

- he makes the continuity from representation learning to large language models
  historically legible
- he helps keep Era 7 tied to research lineage rather than only product
  interfaces
- he gives the modern period a concrete human link between deep learning,
  industrial labs, and large-scale deployment

## Major Contributions In Plain Language

- helped build the research bridge from deep neural-network work into large
  sequence and language models
- worked at Google Brain before becoming an OpenAI co-founder and chief
  scientist
- became one of the clearest person-level anchors for the move from deep
  learning into the foundation-model and LLM period
