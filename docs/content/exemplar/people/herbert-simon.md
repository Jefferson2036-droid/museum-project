# Herbert Simon

## Name

- name: Herbert Simon
- lifespan: 1916-2001

## Role In AI History

Simon matters because he helped frame AI as a science of problem solving and
decision making, while also publicly representing the era's optimism about near-
term machine intelligence.

## Why This Person Matters

- he helps explain why early AI optimism was intellectually serious, not only
  rhetorical
- he connects AI history to bounded rationality, cognition, and problem-solving
  theory
- he links the symbolic era to some of the strongest claims about future machine
  capability

## Major Contributions In Plain Language

- co-created Logic Theorist and General Problem Solver with Allen Newell and
  collaborators
- helped establish heuristic search as a core way to model intelligent problem
  solving
- argued that intelligence could be studied through formal information-
  processing systems

## Tensions, Limitations, Or Misconceptions

  evidence of the era's expectations, but they should not erase his substantive
  methodological contributions
