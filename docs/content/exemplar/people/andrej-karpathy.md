# Andrej Karpathy

## Name

- name: Andrej Karpathy
- lifespan or active period: active from the 2010s through the modern AI era

## Role In AI History

Karpathy is a useful bridge figure between deep-learning research culture and
the public understanding of large language models because he combines early
OpenAI work with unusually influential technical teaching.

## Why This Person Matters

- he helps explain how the foundation-model era spread through education and
  public technical discourse, not only through product launches
- he adds a people-level bridge between OpenAI's early research culture and the
  later mass interest in how LLM systems work
- he gives Era 7 a figure who is historically legible both inside labs and in
  the wider developer and student audience

## Major Contributions In Plain Language

- was a founding member of OpenAI, tying him directly to one of the key
  institutions of the foundation-model era
- became an influential explainer of deep learning and later LLM systems
  through courses, talks, and widely used educational material
- helps make the modern period teachable because his public-facing work shows
  how frontier-model ideas moved into everyday technical culture
