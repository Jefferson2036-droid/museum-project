# Demis Hassabis

## Name

- name: Demis Hassabis
- lifespan or active period: active from the 2010s through the modern AI era

## Role In AI History

Hassabis anchors the modern era's institutional story by tying DeepMind's push
for general AI systems to major breakthroughs in games, language, and science.

## Why This Person Matters

- he helps keep Era 7 from collapsing into a single-company OpenAI narrative
- he makes the current period legible as both a scientific and public-system era
- he represents the leadership line through which DeepMind and Google Brain are
  consolidated into Google DeepMind

## Major Contributions In Plain Language

- co-founded DeepMind and led it as a lab pursuing general AI systems
- helped establish AlphaGo and AlphaFold as two of the most visible modern AI
  breakthroughs outside ordinary chatbot use
- now leads Google DeepMind, linking the DeepMind and Google Brain trajectories
  that shape modern multimodal and foundation-model work
