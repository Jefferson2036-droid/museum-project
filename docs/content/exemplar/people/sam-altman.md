# Sam Altman

## Name

- name: Sam Altman
- lifespan or active period: active from the 2010s through the modern AI era

## Role In AI History

Altman is a key deployment-era figure because he helps turn frontier-model
research into a mass public and economic phenomenon through OpenAI's product,
infrastructure, and AGI framing.

## Why This Person Matters

- he helps explain why Era 7 is not only about model capability but also about
  executive decisions on release, scale, and public interface
- he gives the current period a person-level anchor for the move from research
  lab to globally used AI platform
- he keeps governance, commercialization, and benefit-distribution questions
  visible inside the history rather than outside it

## Major Contributions In Plain Language

- led OpenAI through the public-deployment period that made ChatGPT and later
  multimodal products widely used
- framed iterative deployment, large-scale AI infrastructure, and AGI benefit as
  central public themes of the modern era
- became one of the clearest executive voices shaping how the public imagines
  current and near-future AI capability
