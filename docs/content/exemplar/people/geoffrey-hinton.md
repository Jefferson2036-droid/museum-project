# Geoffrey Hinton

## Name

- name: Geoffrey Hinton
- lifespan or active period: active from the 1970s through the modern AI era

## Role In AI History

Hinton is one of the clearest human anchors for neural-network revival,
representation learning, and the transition from late twentieth-century
connectionism to modern deep learning.

## Why This Person Matters

- he keeps the neural revival visible across multiple decades rather than making
  deep learning look sudden
- he helps connect the 1986 backpropagation story to the later deep-learning
  breakthrough cycle
- he gives the site a human throughline from network skepticism to renewed
  credibility

## Major Contributions In Plain Language

- co-authored the 1986 backpropagation paper that helped restore confidence in
  multi-layer neural-network training
- helped drive renewed interest in deep architectures and learned
  representations in the 2000s
- became one of the best-known public and technical figures of the deep-learning
  era

  technical periods
