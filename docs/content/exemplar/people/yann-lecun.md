# Yann LeCun

## Name

- name: Yann LeCun
- lifespan or active period: active from the 1980s through the modern AI era

## Role In AI History

LeCun helps anchor the practical side of neural networks and deep learning,
especially where learned representations become important in vision and applied
pattern recognition.

## Why This Person Matters

- he helps keep the modern story tied to concrete technical systems rather than
  only high-level trends
- he shows that deep learning's rise depended on work in vision and practical
  machine learning, not only language
- he is one of the clearest links between early neural work and the broader deep
  learning consolidation of the 2010s

## Major Contributions In Plain Language

- helped develop influential convolutional-network approaches for visual and
  pattern-recognition tasks
- became a major public and technical advocate for deep learning and learned
  representations
- co-authored the 2015 deep-learning review that consolidated the period's
  framing
