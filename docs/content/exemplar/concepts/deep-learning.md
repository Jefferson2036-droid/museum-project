# Deep Learning

## Title

- title: Deep learning

## Plain-Language Definition

Deep learning is the study and use of neural models with multiple learned layers
that build increasingly abstract representations from data.

## Why The Concept Matters To AI History

It names the regime that transforms neural networks from one family of methods
into the dominant performance story of the 2010s.

## Historical Emergence Or Turning Point

- deep learning becomes the central field narrative in the 2010s when large
  datasets, improved hardware, and multi-layer models produce major gains in
  image recognition, speech, and language tasks

## Modern Relevance

Deep learning remains the main technical runway into modern transformers,
foundation models, multimodal systems, and large-scale representation learning.

## Common Misconception To Avoid

- deep learning is not simply a synonym for neural networks; historically it
  refers to a later breakthrough regime built on multi-layer learning,
  representation discovery, and scale
