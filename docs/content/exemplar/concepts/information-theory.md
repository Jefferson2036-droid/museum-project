# Information Theory

## Title

- title: Information Theory

## Plain-Language Definition

Information theory studies how signals and messages can be represented,
transmitted, and measured.

## Why The Concept Matters To AI History

It gives the era a formal language for information that reshapes how engineers
and researchers think about communication and machine systems.

## Historical Emergence Or Turning Point

- Shannon's 1948 paper is the core turning point for this concept in the slice

## Modern Relevance

Modern AI relies heavily on information-rich data pipelines, encoding, and
signal-processing assumptions even when the models are very different.

## Common Misconception To Avoid

- information theory is not itself a full theory of intelligence, even though it
  strongly shapes the technical context around AI
