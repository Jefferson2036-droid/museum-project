# Theorem Proving

## Title

- title: Theorem proving

## Plain-Language Definition

Theorem proving uses formal rules to show that a conclusion follows from stated
premises.

## Why The Concept Matters To AI History

It gave early AI one of its clearest demonstrations that machines could perform
forms of reasoning once treated as deeply intellectual human work.

## Historical Emergence Or Turning Point

- theorem proving becomes a defining symbolic-AI activity in the late 1950s and
  1960s through Logic Theorist, resolution methods, unification, and related
  automated reasoning work

## Modern Relevance

It still shapes formal verification, SAT and SMT solving, proof assistants, and
other systems where correctness or explicit reasoning matters more than pattern
matching alone.

## Common Misconception To Avoid

- theorem proving should not be confused with all of AI; it was one powerful
  branch of symbolic work, not the total content of the field
