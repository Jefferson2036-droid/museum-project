# Search And Heuristics

## Title

- title: Search and heuristics

## Plain-Language Definition

Search explores possible solution paths, while heuristics are practical rules
for deciding which paths are more promising than others.

## Why The Concept Matters To AI History

This concept explains why early AI could solve some hard-looking problems: it
did not examine every possibility blindly, but tried to use structured guidance
to search intelligently.

## Historical Emergence Or Turning Point

- search and heuristic problem solving become central in the late 1950s through
  Logic Theorist, GPS, and later planning work such as A\* and STRIPS

## Modern Relevance

Search remains foundational in routing, planning, optimization, games, and many
AI systems that still need explicit exploration rather than pure statistical
prediction.

## Common Misconception To Avoid

- heuristics are not a sign that the system is "cheating"; in AI history they
  are one of the main ways researchers tried to make hard reasoning problems
  tractable
