# Formal Logic

## Title

- title: Formal Logic

## Plain-Language Definition

Formal logic studies reasoning as a symbolic structure that can be represented,
manipulated, and checked by explicit rules.

## Why The Concept Matters To AI History

It gives the prehistory of AI a disciplined way to think about reasoning before
that reasoning is connected to machines.

## Modern Relevance

Modern AI still inherits symbolic structure, representation, and rule-governed
reasoning even when current systems are not purely symbolic.

## Common Misconception To Avoid

- formal logic is not the whole history of AI, but it is one of the clearest
  precursors to later computation and machine reasoning claims
