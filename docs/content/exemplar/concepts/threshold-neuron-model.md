# Threshold-Neuron Model

## Title

- title: Threshold-Neuron Model

## Plain-Language Definition

The threshold-neuron model treats simple neuron-like units as components that
can switch on or off and participate in logical network behavior.

## Why The Concept Matters To AI History

It gives the exemplar slice an early bridge between neurobiology and
computation.

## Historical Emergence Or Turning Point

- the 1943 McCulloch-Pitts paper is the key turning point here

## Modern Relevance

This concept helps students understand that later neural-network systems have a
deep history, even if the modern models are far more complex.

## Common Misconception To Avoid

- the threshold-neuron model should not be described as if it already were a
  modern deep-learning system
