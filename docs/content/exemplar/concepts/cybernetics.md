# Cybernetics

## Title

- title: Cybernetics

## Plain-Language Definition

Cybernetics studies control, feedback, and communication in machines and living
systems.

## Why The Concept Matters To AI History

It helps the exemplar slice show that early AI-adjacent thinking involved
control, systems, and feedback, not just symbolic reasoning.

## Historical Emergence Or Turning Point

- cybernetics becomes a visible context for interpreting machine and neural
  systems during the 1940s and 1950s
