# Mechanized Calculation

## Title

- title: Mechanized Calculation

## Plain-Language Definition

Mechanized calculation is the idea that symbolic procedures can be carried out
by machines rather than by human effort alone.

## Why The Concept Matters To AI History

It helps students see that machine intelligence grows out of an older history of
machines executing structured procedures.

## Historical Emergence Or Turning Point

- Babbage and Lovelace are the key anchors for this bounded precursor slice

## Modern Relevance

It gives later eras a clear lineage from symbolic procedure to machine
operation, which makes the jump into computability and AI less abrupt.

## Common Misconception To Avoid

- mechanized calculation is not yet the same thing as machine intelligence, but
  it is part of the path that makes machine intelligence thinkable
