# Neural Networks

## Title

- title: Neural networks

## Plain-Language Definition

Neural networks are computational systems built from connected units that learn
patterns by adjusting internal weights across layers or other structured
connections.

## Why The Concept Matters To AI History

They give the chronology a long neural thread from early abstraction to modern
learning systems, while showing that the decisive modern change comes from later
training methods and scale rather than from the idea of a network alone.

## Historical Emergence Or Turning Point

- early neural abstraction appears in Era 2, but neural networks become a major
  modern force only after the late-twentieth-century revival of trainable
  multi-layer systems

## Modern Relevance

Neural networks remain the core architecture family behind deep learning,
transformers, modern speech and vision systems, and the later foundation-model
era.

## Common Misconception To Avoid

- neural networks are not identical to deep learning; they are the broader model
  family, while deep learning refers to the later regime of multi-layer training,
  learned representations, and scale
