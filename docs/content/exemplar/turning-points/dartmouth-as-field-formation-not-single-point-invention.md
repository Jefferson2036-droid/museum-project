# Dartmouth As Field Formation, Not Single-Point Invention

## Title

- title: Dartmouth as field formation, not single-point invention

## Core Question Or Conflict

Should Dartmouth be taught as the birth of AI, or as the moment when a longer
runway of ideas was named and consolidated into a field?

## Why It Matters

This turning-point unit keeps the exemplar package from collapsing into a
misleading origin myth.

## Positions Or Pressures Involved

- Dartmouth can look like a clean origin story because it names the field
- the research runway before Dartmouth makes that simple story historically too
  thin
- students need a narrative that honors both the naming moment and the prior
  groundwork
